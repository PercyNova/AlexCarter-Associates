Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of a consultation?

Our initial consultation is free of charge. We’ll discuss your case and provide a clear fee structure for any further services.

How long does a consultation last?

Consultations typically last between 30 minutes to 1 hour, depending on the complexity of your case.

What should I bring to the consultation?

Please bring any relevant documents, such as contracts, court papers, or correspondence related to your case.

Can I schedule a virtual consultation?

Yes, we offer in-person, phone, and video consultations for your convenience.

Do you offer emergency consultations?

Yes, we provide urgent consultations if your situation requires immediate attention. Please contact us to schedule an appointment.

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling our office, filling out the online form, or emailing us directly.

Is there a fee for follow-up consultations?

Follow-up consultations may incur a fee depending on the services provided. We’ll inform you of any costs in advance.

Can I get legal advice over the phone?

Yes, we offer phone consultations for advice on legal matters.

What is your policy on confidentiality?

All information shared with us during consultations is strictly confidential and protected by attorney-client privilege.

Do you accept credit cards for payment?

Yes, we accept major credit cards for payment, in addition to other forms of payment such as checks and bank transfers.

How can I cancel or reschedule a consultation?

You can cancel or reschedule your consultation by calling us or sending an email at least 24 hours in advance.

What areas of law do you specialize in?

We specialize in personal injury, family law, criminal defense, and business law. Please contact us for a consultation to discuss your specific legal needs.
